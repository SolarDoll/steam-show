The Company

Steam Show is a touring spectacle company specialising in fire, LED and large-scale character performance. From signature mechanical dragons to choreographed light, every program is custom-built for the venue and the moment.

Festivals, royal events, brand launches and arena nights — five programs, one obsessive standard of production.
